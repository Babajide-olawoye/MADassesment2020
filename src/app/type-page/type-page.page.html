Shounen

Shounen is by far my favourite type of anime. These anime include One Piece, Dragon Ball and my all time favourite Naruto. On the left is a youtube vid on top 10 shounen shows (based on "Watch Mojo" view). I personally disagree with the list but find it quite accurate to my top shounen list.

Slice of Life

Slice of life is another great genre of anime. When I watch these types of anime I can somewhat relate to them because they usually based on high school or middle school experiances. A good example would be Toradora, Yahari Ore no Seishun Love Comedy wa Machigatteiru, and Haganai. These animes are also quite funny, so I would 100% recommend these anime.

Romance

Romance anime's have some great story that usually involve love triangle. Personally I enjoy watching a lot of these anime and would happily binge watch tham again. My all time favourite anime on this list, that it's also Watch Mojo's number 1 choice of romantic anime, is Toradora. Between me and you this anime actually left me in tears and I'm not a guy who shows his emotions. If you want to hear more about Toradora click here. Anyway back to romance anime, the best romance anime also includes comedy and are set in high school.
